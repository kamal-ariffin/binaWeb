Simple workflow

The 3-step path from case setup to decision support

Start narrow. Capture the engineering case, model uncertainty, and produce a recommendation your team can actually use.

1

Create the case

Define the project, material assumptions, loads, geometry notes, boundary conditions, and baseline outputs.

2

Apply fuzzy uncertainty

Model uncertain inputs with triangular or trapezoidal membership rules and compare result spread against the deterministic baseline.

3

Export the recommendation

Share sensitivity rankings, likely failure drivers, confidence ranges, and next-step actions in a report ready for review.
